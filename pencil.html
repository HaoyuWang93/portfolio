TOKYO PENCIL BUILDING

Despite the Post-Bubble decline of birth and marriage rate, Tokyo sustains a thriving rental market for the city’s education and employment opportunities continuously drawing young (single) migrants. The demand for independent and affordable space shines lights on pencil building:
A vernacular typology of tall buildings standing on tiny footprints in high-density blocks.
