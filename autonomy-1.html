SUBURBIA 2050



Although it is hard to move beyond contemporary progresses and goals when talking about futures, artificial intelligence is offering a promising medium to land our visions on the 'level 5' autonomous systems of mobility and material production. In Southern California where people live upon vehicular traffics in the infinite suburbia, these visions are deeply interwoven with advanced services of a shared economy. The proliferation of shared AV (autonomous vehicles) on roads will allow designers to optimize space usage, not only the street layouts but also personals ones without the needs of parking.




SITE CONDITION - NORTHERN COACHELLA VALLEY

Stacked freeway interchanges, snaking aqueducts, and sprawling suburban communities...these canonical urban archetypes constantly shape and compete for space under Southern California's dynamic ecologies, rendering Coachella Valley as a focal point of speculative developments. The high year-round temperatures contributes high value agricultural specialty crops, while the northern Valley informs a future energy hub for being the second windiest place in the country with consistent sunshine. Today, the Coachella Valley has some of the highest population growth rates in California with its vast northwestern lands remain unincorporated for future forms of suburbian communities.
