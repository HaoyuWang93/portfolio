About


Haoyu Wang is an architect and artistic researcher with an active interest in alternative possibilities of built environments. In addition to shaping future places by design, he has been developing a multimedia genre of architectural fiction that combines design research with digital storytelling to reimagine the predefined and forgotten forms of space, whether they be unrealized utopia from architectural history, dominant sci-fi tropes of popular media, vanishing traditions of marginalized communities, or mundane scenes of our everyday routines. This portfolio skims over Haoyu's journey of observation, investigation, and imagination.
