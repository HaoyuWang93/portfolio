About


Haoyu Wang is an architect and urbanist with an active interest in reimagining the spatial relationships of human conditions. In addition to shaping future places through design, he develops a multimedia genre of architectural fiction that renders alternative urban images to communicate contemporary risks and uncertainties. The aim is to turn architectural thoughts into an artistic instrument of public imagination for cultural repercussions in wider audiences beyond the institutional gazes of design communities. This portfolio skims over some of Haoyu's academic and personal works that have been preparing knowledge and methods toward the multimedia production of architectural fiction.
