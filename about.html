About


Haoyu Wang is a designer and urbanist with an active interest in the concept of architectural fiction: the mental and visual construction of fictional scenarios, activities, and processes in architectural discourse. In addition to developing the concept in the contextual negotiation and design production of architectural works, Haoyu is passionate about its cultural capacity in addressing challenges, raising awareness, and provoking discussion on cities and human conditions. This portfolio skims over some of his academic and personal works toward architectural fiction.
