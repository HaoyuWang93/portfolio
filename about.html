About


Haoyu Wang is an architect and urbanist with an active interest in the spatial relationships of human conditions and their impacts on the haunted futures of contemporary imagination. In addition to shaping better places by design, he has been developing an characteristics genre of architectural fiction that combines speculative design and visual storytelling to overpass the discursive scenarios of contemporary imagination for radical impulses on alternative futures beyond institutional interests. This portfolio skims over some of Haoyu's academic and personal works of observation, investigation, and imagination.
